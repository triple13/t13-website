FAQ

Q.

Who are we?

A.

Triple13 are AMP monetisation specialists that provide programmatic Prebid and optimisation technology solutions.

Q.

Eligibility criteria for new publisher partners?

A.

Triple13 work with both direct digital publishers and advertising technology providers globally.

Q.

What advertisers do Triple13 work with?

A.

Triple13 works with all the leading advertising partners including Google AdExchange, Amazon, Magnite (formally Rubicon Project), OpenX, Xandr, Pubmatic, AdForm to name a few.

Q.

What are the payment terms?

A.

Triple13 uses an automated payment system on 60 day terms.

Q.

What currencies payments are offered?

A.

A publisher may choose their preferred payment currency between GBP, USD or EUR.

Q.

What CPM's can I expect?

A.

Triple13’s AMP monetisation platform compliments its extensive industry experience working alongside publisher partners to maximise yield and drive site performance that in turn attracts higher advertiser CPM’s. Learn more .

Q.

Does Triple13 work on other inventory types than AMP?

A.

Yes! Triple13 monetise AMP, Display, App and Video inventory using our comprehensive multi-platform Advertising partners.

Q.

What is Triple13's relationship with Google?

A.

Triple13 are a trusted partner of Google under the MCM (Multiple Customer Management) umbrella.

Q.

How can I block categories or specific advertisers?

A.

Brand safety is principal; please inform us of any ad categories, brands or specific creatives you wish to block and we will do the rest of the work.

Q.

Is there Technical support?

A.

Yes! The Triple13 team comprises a skilled team of developers and technical analysts working alongside your dedicated account manager to resolve any issues as they arise.  Triple13 also offers bespoke consultancy to suit your specific business needs surrounding AMP and Prebid development and monetisation strategy.

Q.

Do you offer private marketplaces?

A.

Triple13’s demand comprises: Open Exchange, Open Bidding [EBDA] , Server-Side and Client Side Header Bidding, SSP’s and PMP opportunities.

Q.

How long should a test run?

A.

30 days for the optimum results, however publishers can usually expect more instantaneous results upon full implementation of Triple13’s bespoke strategy recommendations.

Q.

What countries can you monetise?

A.

Triple13 montises globally with focussed strengths within UK, USA & English speaking countries.
