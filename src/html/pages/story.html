Developed by a large community of professionals and backed by Google, the AMP framework works perfectly with all browsers and integrates flawlessly with the most widely used content management systems (CMS), making it a viable alternative for companies seeking to adapt their websites to these mobile-friendly times


Mobile has been slowly overtaking desktop in terms of usage for all web related activities. As of 2018, mobile accounted for over 50% of usage worldwide, finally surpassing desktop at 45%

Why AMP?


It's all about speed with accelerated mobile pages. The whole project was built around the premise that mobile users need results faster, so that smartphone-adjusted sites load at optimum rates.

Structured data AMP pages will appear in the Top stories carousel, host carousel of rich results, Visual stories, and rich results in mobile search results. Photos, page icons, and other interesting search result features can be included in these results.

Lower Bounce Rate


According to recent Google study, smartphone users not only demand short loading times, but their expectations still play a significant role in their decision to wait.


According to their research, load times of up to three seconds have a bounce rate of more than 30%. That bounce boost increases to a whopping 90% after five seconds. The average exceeds 100 percent at the six-second mark.
