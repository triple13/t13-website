Empowering data-driven decisions

We empower publishers to compete in a competitive programmatic environment that enables them to better understand their data, enabling them to make faster and more effective decisions.

Programmatic advertising connects with multiple technologies so it can be a challenge to organise and make sense of all the data from multiple sources and to make informed decisions based on your data.

Leya is our in-house proprietary technology that collects all your advertising data into one area. It’s capable of ingesting and analysing terabytes of data from multiple technologies to give you the data you need to manage your campaigns.

It’s hosted on Google’s cloud platform that’s secure (AES256 encryption) and incredibly fast so we can guarantee 99.99% uptime.

There’s no more shifting between different platforms or multiple tabs open from a variety of dashboards. It’s all of your data, all in one place, all in real-time.s.

We made it convenient to start using Leya, there are no long-term contracts or hoops you need to go through. We provide a pay-as-you-go, pricing model that allows you the versatility and autonomy you need without any of the difficulties.
