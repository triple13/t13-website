Trusted partner in AMP monetisation

Accelerated Mobile Pages is an open-source web framework and publishing platform developed by Google in 2015. It helps you to build a simplified version of your web pages, providing super quick loading times and increased user experience as a result. At its heart, AMP stands for a stripped-down version of HTML built to be incredibly lightweight and simple to use.

Simply put, this implies that advertisers can run ads that load quicker and boost engagement by providing improved user functionality while maintaining key monetisation capabilities.

Advantages of using AMP

User Experience

Speed means better loading times and as a result better user experience. It impacts how long visitors spend on your site meaning the more time they spend engaging with your content. This is a key factor for long term success of your site and increased ad revenue from your properties.

Ease of use

With mobile usage on the rise and outstripping users on desktops means we have to do more with limitations of bandwidth. This is where AMP really shines but also how easy it is to implement.

Open source

Being open source means there is a wealth of resources available between publishers, developers and tech companies. There is a whole community that supports the advancement of AMP that includes publishers, platforms, advertisers, creators and users alike.

SEO benefits

It’s common knowledge that faster loading pages is a factor that Google considers when it looks to rank your pages in it’s search results. Also it’s worth noting that since AMP is a Google product there will naturally be a bias towards its value in comparison to other non amp sites which could lead to better rankings.
